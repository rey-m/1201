Technology Opportunities

Usage of this technology rewarded with loyalty bonuses, discounts in favorite areas, split payment between you and your friends and special deals for usage of new technology (AT hop cards). Minimalize the spread of viruses and diseases as money is known to be very good at transmitting diseases(Time, 2017.). AT hop cards, which contains the microchips, are meant to last 6 years durability-wise and give discounts to students, hoping for more people to use public transport rather than their own way of travel, such as through driving.

Technology Risks

If you were to lose or damage your transport card containing said microchip. As for environmental concerns, plastic is used in producing cards.

Technology Choices

There are many choices available when choosing this method of transport Tags, keychains and cards. You can make the choice in which you prefer. Use different mode of transport like buses, trains, scooters, bikes, carpool, and more. Able to change to this technology instead of the other older alternatives. Choice for an easier way of transport and more accessibility. AT Hop made the movement in 2017 to encourage people to leave the cars and use buses, this would lead to the increase in Hop card usage, showing the importance of microchips and there usage.
